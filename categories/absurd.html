Absurd dreams

Creativity

Whenever we dream, weird things can occur without even noticing. Certainly a flying tiger can pass by and it will all seem normal. This might mean that you are a very creative person. Your mind will make up these unexisting creatures, because of your creative and endless thougths.

Chaotic

You might also be a bit chaotic when your dreams are absurd and unrealistic. This is because all your thoughts will merge together into a creative mess. It tells you that you are creative but should do a little cleaning up in de mind. Maybe meditating will make you feel better.
