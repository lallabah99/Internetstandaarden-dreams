Dreams about loved ones

Happiness

Dreaming about loved ones in a positive way may just mean that you are happy the way everything is going. Enjoy these kind of drearms. It shows you that you are a loving and kind person and you might want to tell your loved ones you love them more often.

Fear

Dreaming about loved ones can also mean you are afraid of losing someone important. Or feeling distance. If this is the case try contacting this person and letting know you care about them and want to get closer again.
